George Brown College Overview

George Brown College is one of the largest colleges in Canada, located in the heart of downtown Toronto. With three campuses and a variety of programs, the college emphasizes practical, hands-on learning and close ties with industry leaders.
George Brown College also offers international student programs and is known for its hands-on, industry-focused learning environments. The college has strong ties to businesses, allowing students to gain practical experience through work placements.
George Brown College operates across three main campuses: St. James Campus, Casa Loma Campus, and Waterfront Campus. Each campus focuses on specific programs, offering students specialized resources and environments to support their learning experience.
George Brown College offers a wide range of student support services, including academic advising, career services, mental health counseling, and tutoring. These services help ensure that students have the resources they need to succeed both academically and personally.
