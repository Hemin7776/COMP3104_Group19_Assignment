George Brown College is a public college located in Toronto, Ontario, offering a wide range of programs in arts, business, and technology, including hands-on training and real-world experience.
The college offers various student services, including career coaching, health services, and student clubs.
George Brown College has three main campuses: St. James, Casa Loma, and Waterfront, each offering specialized programs and services.
