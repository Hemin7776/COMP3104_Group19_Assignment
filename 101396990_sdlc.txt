Importance of DevOps in SDLC

DevOps integrates development and operations to improve collaboration, automate processes, and ensure high-quality software delivery. Learning DevOps as part of SDLC helps teams achieve faster delivery and better quality control.
Key Benefits of DevOps in SDLC:

- Faster time to market through automation and continuous delivery.
- Improved collaboration between development and operations teams.
- Higher software quality with early defect detection and resolution.
The Role of Automation in DevOps and SDLC:

- Automation helps reduce manual errors.
- Speeds up repetitive tasks such as testing and deployment.
- Ensures consistency across development and production environments.
