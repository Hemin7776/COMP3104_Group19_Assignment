Programs and Hands-On Learning

Emphasis is on hands-on learning through internships and industry partnerships.
Campuses

The college has three main campuses: St. James, Casa Loma, and Waterfront, all located in downtown Toronto.
Student Support and Diversity

The college provides services like career counseling and academic support for its diverse student body, including international students.
