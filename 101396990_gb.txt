George Brown College Overview

George Brown College, founded in 1967, is a public college in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. It offers over 160 programs, including certificates, diplomas, degrees, and postgraduate certificates.
Programs Offered at George Brown College:

- Business Administration
- Health Sciences
- Information Technology
- Culinary Arts
- Engineering Technologies
Campuses of George Brown College:

- St. James Campus
- Casa Loma Campus
- Waterfront Campus
Student Services at George Brown College:

- Career services for job placement.
- Academic support for tutoring and writing assistance.
- Counseling services to support student well-being.
