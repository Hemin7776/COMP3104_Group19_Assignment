"George Brown College offers a wide variety of programs across various disciplines."
George Brown College is a public college of applied arts and technology.
It offers over 160 full-time programs and 1,500 continuing education courses.
The college has a strong focus on hands-on learning and industry partnerships.
George Brown College has three main campuses: Casa Loma, St. James, and Waterfront.
The college is well-known for its innovative and career-focused programs.
It collaborates with many local and international industries to provide real-world learning experiences.
